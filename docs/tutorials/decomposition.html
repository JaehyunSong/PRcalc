Two-step decomposition

\(\alpha\)-divergence (\(D^\alpha\)) can be decomposed in two components—apportionment and districting as follow:

\[ \begin{align} D^{\alpha} = & \sum_{j = 1}^{k} v_j \frac{1}{\alpha (\alpha - 1)} \log{\frac{s_j}{v_j}} + \\ & \sum_{j=1}^{k} (s_j)^\alpha (v_j)^{1 - \alpha} \sum_{i=1}^{k_j} v_{ji} \frac{1}{\alpha (\alpha - 1)} \Biggl[\biggl(\frac{s_{ji}}{v_{ji}}\biggr)^{\alpha} - 1 \Biggr]\\\ = & D^{\alpha}_{\mbox{apportionment}} + \sum_{j=1}^k W^{\alpha}_j D^{\alpha}_{\mbox{districting}_j}. \end{align} \]

However there are two special cases, \(\alpha \rightarrow 0\) and \(\alpha \rightarrow 1\).

\[ \begin{align} D^0 = & \sum_{j = 1}^{k} v_j \log{\frac{v_j}{s_j}} + \sum_{j=1}^{k} v_j \sum_{i=1}^{k_j} v_{ji} \log{\frac{v_{ji}}{s_{ji}}} \\ = & D^0_{\mbox{apportionment}} + \sum_{j=1}^k W^0_j D^0_{\mbox{districting}_j}. \end{align} \]

\[ \begin{align} D^1 = & \sum_{j = 1}^{k} s_j \log{\frac{s_j}{v_j}} + \sum_{j=1}^{k} s_j \sum_{i=1}^{k_j} s_{ji} \log{\frac{s_{ji}}{v_{ji}}} \\ = & D^1_{\mbox{apportionment}} + \sum_{j=1}^k W^1_j D^1_{\mbox{districting}_j}. \end{align} \]

nz_decompose_all <- nz_district |> 
  group_by(year) |> 
  nest() |> 
  mutate(data      = map(data, \(x) as_prcalc(x,
                                              l1   = "region",

                                              q    = "magnitude",
                                              type = "district")),
         decompose = map(data, \(x) decompose(x, 
                                              alpha      = 0,
                                              special    = "Maori"))) |> 
  pull(decompose)
